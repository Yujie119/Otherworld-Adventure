## 本批范围

本批绘制《Black Myth: Wukong / 黑神话：悟空》不超过 6 个热门官方角色或人物型 boss。每个角色只绘制官方常态代表外观，不绘制同人变体、MOD、非官方服装或非官方配色。

纳入清单：

- The Destined One / 天命人：形态01_常态
- Sun Wukong / The Great Sage's Broken Shell / 孙悟空/大圣残躯：形态01_常态
- Zhu Bajie / 猪八戒：形态01_常态
- Erlang Shen / 二郎神：形态01_常态
- Bull King / 牛魔王：形态01_常态

未纳入 Black Bear Guai、Yellowbrow、Yellow Wind Sage、Princess Rakshasi 等。它们均可作为后续“妖王/Boss”批次候选；本批名额限制为 6 个，优先主角、悟空本体/残躯、主要伙伴和终盘高辨识度人物型 boss。

## 通用风格模板

Create ONE full-body 2D pixel-art game sprite of the official Black Myth: Wukong character in normal in-game form, using the Wukong sprite shown earlier as the visual style and stance reference: compact 256x256-ready pixel-art standing portrait, front or front 3/4 facing viewer, feet near bottom anchor, crisp dark outline, hard-edged chunky pixel clusters, low anti-aliasing, readable Chinese myth dark-fantasy costume details, not smooth high-resolution illustration.

Composition: centered full body, front torso visible, both shoulders visible, contained inside the frame with generous padding. Keep staffs, polearms, horns, capes, tails, beads, armor flags, ribbons, hair, and effects close to the body. No extra characters, no text anywhere.

Background: perfectly flat solid #00ff00 chroma-key background, one uniform color, no shadows, no gradients, no texture, no floor plane. Do not use #00ff00 anywhere in the character. No watermark, no signature.

Negative constraints: No text, no Chinese characters, no kanji, no letters, no numbers, no symbols, no printed marks, no UI, no speech bubbles, no watermark, no signature. Do not draw a back view, rear cloak, pure side profile, or turned-away head. Do not include a scene background, floor, cast shadow, smoke cloud, fire aura, health bar, boss name text, or extra character. If a clothing mark may look like writing, simplify or omit it.
