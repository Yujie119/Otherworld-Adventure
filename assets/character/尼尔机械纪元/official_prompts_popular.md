# 尼尔机械纪元热门角色像素立绘提示词

访问日期：2026-07-07

## 本批范围

本批绘制《NieR:Automata / 尼尔：机械纪元》不超过 4 个热门官方角色，每个角色只绘制官方常规代表形态：

- 2B：形态01_常态
- 9S：形态01_常态
- A2：形态01_常态
- Pascal：形态01_常态

未纳入 Adam/Eve、Commander、Operators、Emil 等官方角色。本批名额限制为不超过 4 个，优先覆盖三名主线核心人形角色和一个辨识度很高的机械生命体代表。未采用同人、MOD、非官方换装或活动皮肤。

## 通用风格模板

Create ONE full-body 2D pixel-art game sprite of the official NieR:Automata character in the specified normal form, using the Wukong sprite shown earlier as the visual style and stance reference: compact 256x256-ready pixel-art standing portrait, front 3/4 facing viewer, feet near bottom anchor, crisp dark outline, hard-edged chunky pixel clusters, low anti-aliasing, readable anime costume details, not smooth high-resolution illustration.

Composition: centered full body, front torso visible, both shoulders visible, contained inside the frame with generous padding. Keep weapons, hair, capes, pods, or character accessories close to the body. No extra characters, no text anywhere.

Background: perfectly flat solid #00ff00 chroma-key background, one uniform color, no shadows, no gradients, no texture, no floor plane. Do not use #00ff00 anywhere in the character. No watermark, no signature.

Negative constraints: No text, no letters, no numbers, no symbols, no UI, no speech bubbles, no watermark, no signature. Do not draw a back view, rear cloak, side profile, or turned-away head. Do not include a scene background, floor, cast shadow, or extra character.
