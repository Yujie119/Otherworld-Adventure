## 通用风格模板

Create ONE full-body 2D pixel-art game sprite of the official Elden Ring monster in its normal in-game form, using the Wukong sprite shown earlier as the visual style and stance reference: compact 256x256-ready pixel-art standing portrait, front or front 3/4 facing viewer, grounded bottom anchor, crisp dark outline, hard-edged chunky pixel clusters, low anti-aliasing, readable dark fantasy silhouette, not smooth high-resolution illustration.

Composition: centered full body or full creature body, contained inside the frame with generous padding. For large quadrupeds, insects, serpentine or many-limbed monsters, use a compact front 3/4 showcase pose so the creature is readable and not too wide. Keep claws, wings, tails, roots, tentacles, weapons, cages, arms, and effects close to the body. No extra characters, no text anywhere.

Background: perfectly flat solid #ff00ff chroma-key background, one uniform color, no shadows, no gradients, no texture, no floor plane. Do not use #ff00ff anywhere in the monster. No watermark, no signature.

Negative constraints: No text, no letters, no numbers, no symbols, no UI, no speech bubbles, no watermark, no signature. Do not draw a back view, pure side profile, or turned-away head. Do not include a scene background, floor, cast shadow, extra enemy, health bar, item icon, rune symbol, or boss name text.
