## 通用风格模板

Create ONE full-body 2D pixel-art game sprite of the official Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice character in normal in-game form, using the Wukong sprite shown earlier as the visual style and stance reference: compact 256x256-ready pixel-art standing portrait, front or front 3/4 facing viewer, feet near bottom anchor, crisp dark outline, hard-edged chunky pixel clusters, low anti-aliasing, readable Sengoku dark-fantasy costume details, not smooth high-resolution illustration.

Composition: centered full body, front torso visible, both shoulders visible, contained inside the frame with generous padding. Keep swords, spears, prosthetic arms, robes, hats, hair, beads, and weapons close to the body. No extra characters, no text anywhere.

Background: perfectly flat solid #00ff00 chroma-key background, one uniform color, no shadows, no gradients, no texture, no floor plane. Do not use #00ff00 anywhere in the character. No watermark, no signature.

Negative constraints: No text, no kanji, no letters, no numbers, no symbols, no printed marks, no UI, no speech bubbles, no watermark, no signature. Do not draw a back view, rear cloak, pure side profile, or turned-away head. Do not include a scene background, floor, cast shadow, blood splash, boss name text, health bar, or extra character. If a clothing mark may look like writing, simplify or omit it.
